WHAT IS OPENSOLDAT

Soldat is an exciting unique side-view multiplayer action game. Played by hundreds of players at this moment. It takes the best from games like Liero, Worms, Quake and Counter-Strike and gives you fast action gameplay with tons of blood and flesh. Little ragdoll soldiers fight against each other on 2D battle arenas using a deadly arsenal.
This is what multiplayer was invented for.

Pure fun, addictive kill'em all gameplay. NOW OPEN!
